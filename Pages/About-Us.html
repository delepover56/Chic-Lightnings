About us

Lighting with service behind it.

Chic Lighting & Design helps residential and commercial clients choose fixtures that suit the room, the finish palette, and the level of brightness they need every day. From statement chandeliers and pendant lighting to softer lamps, wall lights, and ceiling fixtures, the team focuses on proportion, warmth, durability, and aftercare so each interior feels finished and practical.

Chic Lighting & Design has had its presence since 1971, serving clients who want lighting that feels considered instead of generic. We offer lighting and accessories for eclectic interiors, including LED lights, lamps, chandeliers, pendant lighting, wall lights, and ceiling lights.

Our catalog includes crystal chandeliers, traditional chandeliers, modern chandeliers, glass pendant lights, industrial and retro pendant lights, metal pendant lights, and more.

Customer service is central to the work. During business hours, customers can reach a design professional for guidance on product selection, lighting plans, and aftercare support.
