Wall light planning

Use wall lights where overhead light feels too flat.

Wall lights add direction, rhythm, and soft ambience to indoor lighting plans. They work well beside beds, along corridors, near mirrors, on stair routes, and across feature walls where ceiling fixtures need extra depth.

Choose home decor lights by mounting height, beam direction, finish, and shade material. For a complete room, combine ceiling lights for general LED lighting, modern lamps for task lighting, and chandeliers for statement indoor lighting.
