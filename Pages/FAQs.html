What is Chic Lighting about?

Chic Lighting & Design has had its presence since 1971. We offer unique lighting and accessories from elegant traditional designs to refined contemporary interiors.

What services do you provide?

We specialize in LED lights, lamps, chandeliers, pendant lighting, ceiling lights, wall lights, and support for residential and commercial lighting selection.

What customer support do you offer?

Customers can contact us for design guidance, product details, spare part support, and lighting project assistance during normal business hours.

How can I request product pricing?

Use the contact form and include the product category, quantity, and any room details. The form sends your inquiry to taha82426980@gmail.com.

Can you help me choose a fixture size?

Yes. Share the room dimensions, ceiling height, photos if available, and whether the fixture is for ambient, accent, or task lighting.

Do you support commercial projects?

Yes. We can help with lighting options for hotels, restaurants, offices, studios, and other commercial interiors.
